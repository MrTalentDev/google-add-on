Privacy policy

Easy Rescheduling respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information that you may provide as a user of our Google Calendar add-on. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information that we may collect and how it is used.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect or store any personal information from users of the Easy Rescheduling add-on. The add-on uses Google Calendar API to access your calendar data, but this is done solely for displaying your events and rescheduling them. We do not have access to view or modify your calendar data beyond what is necessary for the functioning of the add-on.

Automated Information

When you use Easy Rescheduling, certain anonymous usage data may be automatically collected by Google Analytics. This includes information such as the type of browser and device you are using, your IP address, the date and time of your visit, and the pages you accessed. This information is used solely for the purposes of improving the add-on and is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Access

Easy Rescheduling does not share any of your personal information with third parties.

Updates to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. Any changes will be posted on this page, and if significant, we will provide a more prominent notice (including, for certain services, email notification of Privacy Policy changes).

Support

Easy Rescheduling Support Page

Thank you for using Easy Rescheduling, the Google add-on that simplifies the rescheduling process for Google Calendar events. Here are some Frequently Asked Questions that might help you:

FAQs

  1. How do I install Easy Rescheduling?

    2. You can easily install Easy Rescheduling by visiting the Google Workspace Marketplace and clicking the "Install" button. Once you have installed it, you can start using it right away!

  2. How do I use Easy Rescheduling?

    3. First, you need to open your Google Calendar and select the event that you want to reschedule. Then, you need to click the "Easy Rescheduling" icon in the right sidebar. This will open the Easy Rescheduling panel where you can select a new date and time for your event. Once you have chosen the new date and time, simply click "ADD EVENT" and your event will be rescheduled!

  3. How can I get more help?

    4. If you still have questions or need further assistance with Easy Rescheduling, please contact our support team at james.cryptodev.1128@gmail.com. Our support team is available 24/7 to help you with any issues or concerns that you may have.
